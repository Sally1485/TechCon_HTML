History of Mayfil TechCon

Mayfil TechCon is a global organization which was founded by Byun Baekhyun. The organization aims to help startup business in the technology industry to grow and cope in this spaces. In 2020 the organiztion went on colloborate different industry players to come on board to meet every year to socialize and create ideas to solve problems facing the world!

Mission

The main mission of TechCon conferences is to bring industry players from all over the world under one roof to socialize with each other, seek and solve each others problem, for young people to meet their mentors and most importantly to colloborate and bring ideas to solve global problems including climate change.
